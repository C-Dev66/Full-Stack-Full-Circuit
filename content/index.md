Hi there, you’ve landed on my notes page.

Over time, I’ve filled countless notebooks with hand written project highlights, brainstorming sessions, key concept summaries, and frequently used code snippets/commands. While this method served me well at first, it quickly became inefficient when I needed to locate a specific piece of information.

This knowledge base is my way of organizing ideas and projects in a polished, searchable format. It also provides an easy way to share insights with colleagues and collaborators who may be interested in the topics I’m exploring.

All notes begin in an Obsidian vault and are then published here through GitHub Pages. Locally, I tag entries with common words or phrases, making them easy to search and link visually. You can browse topics in the Explorer or switch to Graph View to uncover connections across tags and posts.

Enjoy exploring & learn something new everyday!
