# How to Learn PCB Design - Phil's Lab

Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Design is an important skill to have especially if you're an Electrical Engineer. Most university curriculums do not cover this topic. Therefore, it is important that you pick up this skill now. In the past ordering custom PCB's was very costly. Nowadays its affordable to send your schematic to a supplier and have 5+ circuit boards shipped to your home.

The best way to improve your skill in making custom PCBs is to practice and learn by doing. Progressively overload by experimenting and challenging your self with new methods/solutions. Be direct in your learning, reference previous know good examples that can be found online. Leverage getting a second set of eyes on your design, send your PCB for evaluation from a friend or online. Learn from your mistakes and strive to not repeat them in your next design. Don't just design the PCBs also get them manufactured.
