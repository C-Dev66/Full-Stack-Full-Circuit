# How to use OpenRocket to Simulate Model Rocket

OpenRocket is an open source project developed by a graduate student for their master theisis. His goal was to deliver an open platform for individuals in the amateur rocket space a method to simulate their rockets without having the pay the fee of a full license software.

This tutorial will document my journey in modeling the Estes Vogel. Este's first High Power Rocket and my first self built model. To begin the journey we will be using resources from youtube.

If this is your first time running OpenRocket begin by adjusting the Preferences. Most of the components you purchase will be in Imperial because of  Set Units to **Default Imperial**. Start with the Nose Cone, if your purchased a model you can look up the part via the **Choose Component Preset menu**. Next proceed with the body tubes, Tube couplers, bulkhead, shock cord, parachutes, inner tube, centering rings.
